Social Housing Services

Exceptional Social Housing Solutions

At Kooka, we offer exceptional services tailored for social housing projects. Whether you’re a housing association, local council, or social housing developer, our team delivers reliable, high-quality solutions for insulation, rendering, and sustainability.

Our services

At Kooka, we specialise in delivering high-quality insulation and rendering solutions tailored to the social housing sector. Our expertise includes comprehensive services such as External Wall Insulation, Internal Wall Insulation, Cavity Wall Extraction, Silicone Rendering, and Loft Insulation. Currently, we are proud to be part of the Watford Housing scheme, showcasing our commitment to creating sustainable and energy-efficient social housing. Our solutions not only enhance the longevity and aesthetic appeal of housing projects but also significantly improve energy efficiency and occupant comfort. By partnering with us, construction professionals can rely on our innovative techniques, meticulous attention to detail, and dedication to excellence, ensuring that every social housing project is completed to the highest standards. Our deep understanding of the social housing sector’s unique challenges allows us to deliver results that meet both regulatory requirements and the needs of the community.
