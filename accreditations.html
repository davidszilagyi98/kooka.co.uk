Accreditations and Certificates: Our Commitment to Excellence

Our Certifications and Training

At Kooka, we pride ourselves on delivering top-quality services, backed by industry-recognised certifications. Our commitment to excellence is demonstrated through our adherence to the highest standards in the construction and insulation sectors. Each of our certifications reflects our dedication to providing reliable, efficient, and sustainable solutions for our customers. By choosing Kooka, you can trust that our expertise is validated by rigorous industry benchmarks, ensuring that your project is handled with the utmost professionalism and care. Explore our certifications to learn more about our qualifications and the exceptional standards we uphold.

Awards

At Kooka, we’ve made it our mission to set the gold standard in insulation and rendering industry, delivering results that leave a lasting impact. As an award-winning company since 2022, we’ve earned the trust of our customers through a steadfast commitment to innovation, quality, and unmatched service. Our dedication to excellence isn’t just about recognition; it’s about ensuring every project exceeds expectations, both in performance and aesthetics.

We proudly hold a portfolio of certifications and accreditations, reflecting our compliance with the industry’s most stringent standards. These prestigious endorsements aren’t just for show—they’re proof that we adhere to the highest benchmarks of safety, efficiency, and sustainability. Whether it’s reducing energy costs for homeowners or enhancing the durability and beauty of commercial buildings, our work consistently delivers measurable value and lasting benefits.

Government Backed Quality

At Kooka, we proudly hold the TrustMark accreditation, a government-backed quality assurance scheme. TrustMark ensures that businesses meet high standards in workmanship, customer care, and trading practices. Our certification reflects our commitment to delivering outstanding services in insulation and rendering industry, adhering to the best industry practices. By being TrustMark certified, we offer customers peace of mind, knowing that our work is regulated, safe, and in line with government-endorsed quality standards. This accreditation is a mark of reliability and excellence, ensuring your project is in trusted hands.

H&S Standards

Our CHAS Advanced and Constructionline Gold certifications reflect our dedication to meeting the highest Health & Safety (H&S) standards in the industry. CHAS Advanced demonstrates our compliance with rigorous safety regulations, ensuring a secure working environment for both our team and clients. Constructionline Gold, similarly, upholds stringent H&S, environmental, and quality management benchmarks. Together, these accreditations highlight our unwavering commitment to operational excellence, risk management, and maintaining a responsible approach to every project we undertake.

Insulated Render & Cladding Association

Our membership with the Insulated Render and Cladding Association (INCA) reinforces our expertise and commitment to excellence in external wall insulation, rendering, and cladding. INCA represents industry leaders dedicated to high standards, innovation, and sustainability. Being a member ensures that we stay at the forefront of best practices, offering our clients superior solutions that meet industry standards for quality, energy efficiency, and performance. This accreditation strengthens our dedication to enhancing building performance while delivering premium services in the insulation and cladding sector.

Insurance Backed Guarantees

We offer the Qualitymark Protection Insurance Backed Guarantee, providing an extra layer of security for our customers. This guarantee ensures that, in the unlikely event of an issue, your investment in our insulation and rendering services is protected, even if the company ceases trading. Qualitymark’s insurance-backed guarantee reflects our commitment to reliability and long-term customer satisfaction, giving you peace of mind that your project is safeguarded by an independent third party, securing both the quality and durability of our work.

Retrofit Qualifications

Our Elmhurst Energy Retrofit qualification highlights our advanced expertise in delivering sustainable, energy-efficient solutions. Elmhurst Energy is the UK’s leading authority on energy performance, and their certification signifies that we are fully equipped to assess and carry out retrofitting projects that improve building efficiency, reduce energy consumption, and lower carbon footprints. This qualification reflects our commitment to helping clients meet modern energy standards, providing cost-effective, eco-friendly upgrades that enhance building performance while contributing to a more sustainable future.

Energy Efficiency Association Member

As a proud member of the Energy Efficiency Association (EEA), Kooka demonstrates its commitment to advancing energy-saving technologies and sustainable practices. EEA membership signifies that we align with industry-leading standards for promoting energy efficiency in buildings and construction. This association connects us with a network of professionals dedicated to reducing energy consumption, lowering carbon footprints, and creating eco-friendly solutions. Being part of the EEA allows us to stay informed on the latest advancements and ensures that our clients benefit from cutting-edge, energy-efficient services.

Staff Training & Upskilling

We invest in continuous development through the Construction Industry Training Board (CITB), we prioritise the growth and development of our team by actively investing in CITB-certified training and upskilling programs, we ensure our staff stay ahead of industry advancements, mastering new techniques, safety protocols, and technologies. This commitment to continuous professional development not only strengthens our workforce but also ensures that we deliver the highest standards of workmanship and innovation on every project. Through CITB, we equip our team with the skills needed to excel in an ever-evolving construction landscape.

Accredited Installers

We are proud to be an EWIPRO Accredited Installer, a recognition that underscores our expertise in applying advanced external wall insulation systems. EWIPRO certification ensures that we meet the highest standards for installing energy-efficient, high-performance insulation and rendering solutions. This accreditation guarantees that our team is trained and qualified to deliver durable, effective, and sustainable results, providing our clients with peace of mind and assurance that their projects meet industry-leading benchmarks for quality and performance.

Policy Liaison Group for Future Homes

At Kooka, our Director Bobby plays a pivotal role in representing The Group’s Advisory Board and is actively involved with the Policy Liaison Group for Future Homes. This initiative is dedicated to shaping the future of housing through forward-thinking policy development and deep collaboration with key stakeholders. The consortium, which includes government officials, urban planners, architects, and community representatives, works together to tackle emerging trends and challenges in the housing sector. Our participation ensures that Kooka remains a leader in delivering innovative, sustainable housing solutions, while contributing to policies that address the evolving needs of both homeowners and communities. Aligned with our core ethos, we are committed to advancing projects that prioritise sustainability, innovation, and the long-term well-being of future generations. Our active role in these strategic conversations ensures that Kooka’s meet and exceed cutting-edge standards, positioning us at the forefront of the housing industry’s evolution. Through this engagement, we continue to drive positive change, ensuring our work not only reflects the highest standards of modern housing but also anticipates the future needs of society.
