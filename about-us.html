Kooka: Your Premier Award-Winning External Wall Insulation Specialists

With over 30 years of expertise in installing exterior insulation systems, Kooka stands as the leading and award-winning choice for homeowners, developers, and businesses alike. Our comprehensive services include External Wall Insulation, cavity wall extraction, cavity wall insulation, internal wall insulation, silicone render, loft insulation, plastering, drylining, as well as windows and doors installation.

We cater to diverse needs and budgets, collaborating closely with councils to offer tailored solutions. As Stroma Certified Installers, Trustmark Endorsed, and EWI Pro Recommended Installers, we provide a 10-year guarantee, ensuring complete peace of mind for our customers.
