Boost Your Insulation and Render Projects with Funding

Unlock substantial funding opportunities for your insulation projects through the Great British Insulation Scheme (GBIS) with Kooka. GBIS offers generous cavity wall insulation grants and wall insulation grants as part of government-backed initiatives aimed at enhancing energy efficiency and reducing carbon emissions. As a certified provider under GBIS, we specialise in navigating the complexities of grant applications to secure funding that supports your sustainability goals.

Our expert team is dedicated to helping you maximise your investment through tailored funding solutions, ensuring cost-effective and environmentally responsible outcomes. Whether you're upgrading existing properties or incorporating insulation into new builds, our comprehensive understanding of grant funding ensures seamless integration of financial support into your project planning. Partner with Kooka to leverage these grant opportunities and achieve superior insulation solutions that not only meet regulatory standards but also deliver long-term energy savings and enhanced comfort for occupants.
