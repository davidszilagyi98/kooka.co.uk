What is Internal Wall Insulation (IWI)?

Internal Wall Insulation (IWI) involves fitting a high-performance insulating layer to the inside surfaces of your walls. This approach is particularly beneficial for properties with solid walls or where external insulation isn’t an option. By significantly reducing heat loss, IWI enhances your home’s thermal performance, making your indoor space more comfortable and energy efficient.

Internal Wall Insulation: Costs and Frequently Asked Questions

The cost of Internal Wall Insulation (IWI) can vary depending on several factors such as the size of the property, the type of insulation materials used, and the complexity of the job but generally ranges between £80 & £100 per m2. Things like removal/alterations to bathrooms and kitchens can greatly increase this cost.

Internal Wall Insulation is highly effective in improving a property’s thermal performance. By adding a layer of insulation to the interior walls, it reduces heat loss, maintains indoor temperatures, and can significantly lower energy bills. Additionally, it can contribute to a more comfortable living environment by reducing drafts and cold spots.

There are several types of materials commonly used for Internal Wall Insulation, including rigid foam boards, mineral wool, and multi-foil insulation. Each material has its own advantages in terms of thermal performance, fire resistance, and ease of installation. The choice of material will depend on your specific requirements and preferences.

Internal Wall Insulation is a durable solution that can last for many years, often up to 25 years or more. The longevity of the insulation depends on the quality of the materials used and the workmanship of the installation. Properly installed IWI should provide long-term benefits and performance.

When installed correctly, Internal Wall Insulation should not cause damp or condensation problems. However, it is crucial to ensure proper ventilation and moisture management in the property. Contact Kooka today and we will assess your home’s specific conditions and take necessary measures to prevent any potential issues related to dampness or condensation.

Internal Wall Insulation is particularly beneficial for older properties with solid walls that lack cavity insulation. It is also suitable for homes where External Wall Insulation is not feasible due to planning restrictions or aesthetic considerations.
