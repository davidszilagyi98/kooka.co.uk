Our services

Kooka delivers expert insulation and rendering services designed to meet the demands of commercial projects. Whether you're embarking on a new construction or a major renovation, we provide comprehensive solutions including External Wall Insulation to boost energy efficiency, Cavity Wall Insulation for improved thermal performance, and Cavity Wall Extraction for the effective removal of old insulation. Our range of services also includes Internal Wall Insulation for enhanced comfort and soundproofing, Silicone Render for durable and high-quality finishes, and Loft Insulation for effective energy conservation. We also handle Plastering & Drylining for professional interior finishes and offer Windows & Doors solutions for improved security and aesthetics. As a leading choice for developers, property managers, architects, and energy efficiency consultants, Kooka is dedicated to delivering outstanding results on every commercial project.
