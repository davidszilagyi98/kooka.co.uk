Internal Wall Insulation (IWI)

Enhance your interior’s energy efficiency and create a comfortable living environment with our premium Internal Wall Insulation (IWI) solutions. Our IWI systems, featuring SWIP and Ezyfit, are specifically designed to improve thermal performance from the inside, effectively reducing heat loss and lowering energy bills, all while preserving your property's interior aesthetics. These systems are key to contributing to a more sustainable future and ensuring your spaces remain cosy and energy-efficient year-round.

Kooka selects SWIP and Ezyfit for Internal Wall Insulation because these systems perfectly align with our sustainability objectives and commitment to energy efficiency. SWIP’s IWI products, for instance, are engineered to minimise thermal bridging and enhance insulation performance, which substantially reduces energy consumption and carbon emissions. This not only supports environmental preservation but also provides significant cost savings for our clients. Both SWIP and Ezyfit are recognized for their durability and straightforward installation process, making them ideal for long-term performance with minimal maintenance. This ensures that our installations are not only effective but also responsible and sustainable, reflecting Kooka's dedication to superior, eco-friendly building practices.

For more information on the SWIP and Ezyfit systems we utilise, or to explore how our IWI solutions can benefit your property, please visit SWIP's system page, Ezyfit system page or contact us directly.

Cavity Wall Insulation

Boost your building’s energy efficiency and minimise heat loss with our advanced Cavity Wall Insulation (CWI) solutions. Our insulation systems, featuring InstaFibre Supafil 40 and 34, are expertly designed to fill the cavities within your walls, effectively trapping heat inside during the winter and keeping your property cooler during the summer. By choosing our CWI services, you can enhance indoor comfort, significantly reduce energy bills, and lower your carbon footprint.

Kooka uses InstaFibre Supafil 40 and 34 for their Cavity Wall Insulation system due to their exceptional insulating properties and reliable installation process. These materials are specifically engineered to deliver maximum thermal efficiency, ensuring that the insulation remains effective over time without settling or deteriorating. This helps our clients achieve consistent energy savings and contributes to long-term sustainability. Our use of these premium products underscores Kooka's commitment to providing environmentally responsible and high-performing insulation solutions.

Loft Insulation

Elevate your home's energy efficiency and minimise heat loss with our premium Loft Insulation system. By using these systems Kooka is committed to providing Loft Insulation that not only improves comfort but also aligns with our dedication to environmentally responsible practices.
