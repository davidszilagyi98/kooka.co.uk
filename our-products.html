Internal Wall Insulation (IWI)

Internal Wall Insulation (IWI) offers a highly effective solution for improving the thermal performance of buildings from within. By installing insulation materials on the interior walls, IWI helps retain heat within the living space, enhancing energy efficiency and reducing heating costs. It is particularly beneficial for properties where external insulation is impractical or undesirable.
