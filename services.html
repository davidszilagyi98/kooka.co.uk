Services

How can we help you?

External Wall Insulation

At Kooka, we specialise in external wall insulation (EWI) that significantly boosts your home’s energy efficiency and comfort. By adding a protective layer to your exterior walls, our expert solutions reduce heat loss and enhance your property’s aesthetics. With our insulation, you’ll enjoy a warmer home in winter and a cooler one in summer, all while cutting down on energy bills. Trust Kooka to transform your home with our top-quality external wall insulation services

Cavity Wall Insulation

Cavity wall insulation is a crucial home improvement that enhances energy efficiency by filling the gap between your home's inner and outer walls. This cost-effective solution prevents heat loss, reduces energy bills, and enhances indoor comfort throughout the year. At Kooka, our expert team specialises in professional cavity wall insulation services, ensuring your home stays warm in winter and cool in summer. Explore our insulation solutions today and start saving on energy costs while increasing your home's comfort.

Cavity Wall Extraction

Cavity wall extraction is essential for homes with ineffective or damaged insulation. Our expert team at Kooka excels in safely and efficiently removing old or faulty cavity wall insulation. This process restores your wall's integrity and prepares it for new, high-performance insulation. By opting for cavity wall extraction, you can prevent damp issues, improve energy efficiency, and ensure a healthier indoor environment. Trust Kooka for professional cavity wall extraction services that enhance your home’s comfort and energy savings.

Internal Wall Insulation

Internal wall insulation (IWI) is an effective way to enhance your home's energy efficiency and comfort. At Kooka, our skilled team is proficient in installing high-quality internal wall insulation that reduces heat loss, lowers energy bills, and minimises noise. This solution is perfect for homes where external insulation isn't feasible. By insulating your interior walls, you create a warmer environment in winter and a cooler one in summer. Choose Kooka for expert internal wall insulation services that improve your home's efficiency and comfort year-round.

Silicone Render

Silicone render is an advanced solution for enhancing the exterior appearance and durability of your home. At Kooka, our skilled team excels in applying high-quality silicone render that provides a breathable, weather-resistant finish. Recommended as an EWI Pro installer, we ensure top-notch application standards for superior results. This modern render improves your home's aesthetics while protecting against cracking and moisture. Choose silicone render for a long- lasting, low-maintenance exterior that stands up to the elements. Trust Kooka for professional silicone render services that combine beauty and durability

Loft Insulation

Loft insulation is a critical component for enhancing residential energy efficiency and reducing heat loss. At Kooka, we specialise in the meticulous installation of high-quality loft insulation solutions. Our expert team ensures comprehensive coverage and precise application, optimizing thermal performance and minimizing energy expenditures. By choosing Kooka for your loft insulation needs, you benefit from a warmer home in winter, cooler interiors in summer, and significant long-term savings on energy costs. Trust our commitment to professionalism and expertise in delivering superior insulation solutions.

Plastering & Drylining

Plastering and drylining are essential for achieving a flawless interior finish in residential and commercial spaces. Kooka specialise in providing expert plastering and drylining services tailored to meet the highest standards of craftsmanship. Our skilled team ensures precise application and seamless integration, enhancing the aesthetic appeal and structural integrity of your property. Whether you require new installations, repairs, or renovations, Kooka delivers reliable solutions that promise durability and visual excellence. Trust us to transform your spaces with professional plastering and drylining services.

Windows & Doors

Enhance your home's functionality and aesthetic appeal with Kooka expert services in windows and doors. We specialize in the installation of high-quality windows and doors that improve energy efficiency, security, and curb appeal. Our dedicated team ensures meticulous installation, offering a range of styles and materials to suit your preferences and budget.
