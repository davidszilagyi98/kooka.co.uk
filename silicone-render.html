What is Silicone Render?

Silicone Render is a high-performance exterior finish made from silicone-based resins. It provides a durable, weather-resistant, and breathable solution for building facades. Silicone Render: Costs and Frequently Asked Questions

Silicone Render is a high-performance, flexible, and breathable exterior finish made from silicone-based resins. It is designed to enhance the aesthetic appeal and longevity of building facades, offering advantages such as durability, water repellence, and low maintenance.

The key benefits of silicone render include enhanced durability and flexibility, superior water repellence, breathability, self-cleaning properties, UV resistance, aesthetic versatility, improved thermal performance, and long-term value.

Silicone Render is known for its long-lasting durability. When applied correctly by certified professionals, it can last for several decades, maintaining its appearance and protective qualities with minimal maintenance.

Yes, Silicone Render is highly water- repellent. Its hydrophobic surface effectively sheds rainwater, preventing moisture penetration and protecting the building's underlying structure from water damage.

Silicone Render is designed to be low maintenance. Its self-cleaning properties mean that dirt and grime are naturally washed away by rainwater, keeping the facade clean and reducing the need for frequent maintenance.

Silicone Render is versatile and can be applied to a wide range of building types, including residential, commercial, and industrial properties. It is compatible with various substrates such as concrete, brick, and external insulation systems.

Silicone Render is typically applied by professional installers in a multi-layer process. This includes preparing the substrate, applying a base coat, embedding reinforcement mesh, and then applying the Silicone Render as the final coat.

Silicone Render is available in a wide range of colours and textures, offering architects and designers the flexibility to achieve their desired aesthetic. From smooth contemporary finishes to traditional textured looks, there are many options to choose from.

Many Silicone Render products are designed to be environmentally friendly, with low VOC (volatile organic compound) emissions and the ability to enhance a building's energy efficiency by improving thermal performance.

EWI Pro silicone render is renowned for its exceptional quality and advanced technology. As certified EWI Pro installers, we offer a render solution that combines the flexibility and weather resistance of silicone with superior insulation properties, resulting in a beautiful, efficient, and resilient building exterior.

In many cases, Silicone Render can be applied over existing render, provided that the underlying render is sound and stable. However, it is important to assess the condition of the existing render and prepare the surface appropriately before application.
